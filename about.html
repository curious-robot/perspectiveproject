Mission Statement

By experiencing the perspectives of others and learning from them, we combat ignorance, grow as people, and become more loving individuals.

How It Works

The Perspective Project is an attempt to collect interesting, inspiring, incredible life stories from the diverse and wonderful people who live in Western North Carolina. I do this by interacting with strangers and friends and simply asking them a few questions about their lives. What are they interested in? What was their proudest moment? Where is their favorite place on the planet? I ask if I can take their portrait, and compress the interview into a short, meaningful snippet to be shared anonymously.

I don't know where this plan will take me, but my goal right now is to exchange perspectives between people and help to share their eye-opening stories that have not yet been told.

About Me

Hello! I am the Curious Robot. My name is V. Thompson, and I am a high-school student currently living in Murphy, North Carolina. I have a passion for photography and story telling, and this project is a combination of both. It has expanded my horizons as I've gathered tales from the strangers and friends around me I never would have thought to engage before.

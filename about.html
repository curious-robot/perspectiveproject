Mission Statement

By experiencing the perspectives of others and learning from them, we combat ignorance, grow as people, and become more loving individuals.

How It Works

The Perspective Project is an attempt to collect interesting, inspiring, and incredible true stories from the diverse and wonderful people who find themselves in Western North Carolina. I discover these stories by asking a stranger or acquantance a few questions about their lives. What do they remember most about growing up? What was their proudest moment? What is something new they've learned? I ask if I can take their portrait, and compress the conversation into a short, meaningful snippet to be shared anonymously.

The only goal of this project is to create a body of work that exchanges perspectives, opens minds, and preserves unique tales.

About Me

Hello! My name is Verle Thompson, and I am a high-school student currently living in Murphy, North Carolina. I have a passion for photography and story telling, and this project is a combination of both. This adventure has put me out of my comfort zone and helped me to understand people more fully. I highly recommend talking to someone who is different from you this week with the intention of learning something from them and seeing things from their perspective.
